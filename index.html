Interaction
as Identity

The project develops practical tools and methods for UI/UX practitioners to systematically design branded interactions. Starting with micro-interactions like buttons and loading states, and by manipulating interaction parameters through code, I'm exploring how interface behaviors can make brands feel distinctive without relying on visual identity such as logos or colors.
